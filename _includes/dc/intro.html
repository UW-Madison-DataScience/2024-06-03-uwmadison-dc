The goal of this workshop is to provide an introduction to core geospatial data concepts and dive into working with raster/vector data, including: how to open, work with, and plot vector and raster-format spatial data in R. Additional topics include working with spatial metadata (extent and coordinate reference systems), reprojecting spatial data, and working with raster time series data.
